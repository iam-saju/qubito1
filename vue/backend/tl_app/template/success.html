how to get chat-id

1. Click the bot link ->qubit-bot to connect Telegram bot chat

2. Tap the “Start” button at the bottom of the chat screen to initiate a conversation with the bot.

3. Now, send any random messages to the bot(atleast 3) — they can be anything

4. After sending those messages, the bot will now have your chat ID-url from its end.

5. the chat-id will be enclosed in a json-format
